Housing

Student Housing Designed Around Your Program

Accommodation is not just a logistical requirement. It influences student wellbeing, confidence and the success of the academic experience.

Housing Philosophy

Where students live matters.

We help universities identify accommodation options that support program objectives, student profile and duration. Each solution is selected with care and aligned with the broader student experience.

Accommodation Options

Flexible housing solutions for different program needs.

University Residences

Ideal for student groups seeking independence and community.

Homestays

For deeper Spanish language practice and cultural immersion.

Shared Apartments

Appropriate for longer programs, graduate students or researchers.

Hotels

Suitable for faculty, executives and short professional programs.

  • Reservation coordination
  • Arrival and check-in support
  • Communication during the program
  • Check-out and departure coordination
