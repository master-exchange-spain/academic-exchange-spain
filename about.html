About

A Trusted Partner for Universities in Spain

Academic Exchange Spain is a boutique international education consultancy dedicated to designing tailor-made academic experiences for universities from around the world.

Our Story

Created to support meaningful academic experiences in Spain.

Academic Exchange Spain was created with a clear purpose: to help universities develop meaningful academic experiences in Spain through personalized planning, trusted local partnerships and careful coordination.

Rather than offering standard itineraries, we work alongside each institution to create programs that reflect its curriculum, learning outcomes and international strategy.

Our Philosophy

We don't offer standard programs. We design academic experiences around your university.

Academic Excellence

Educational quality is the foundation of every program we design.

Institutional Partnership

We work as an extension of your international office.

Student Wellbeing

A positive student experience contributes to meaningful learning.

Personalized Service

Every university deserves flexible solutions and individual attention.

What We Do

Complete academic support for university programs.

  • Customized Academic Programs

  • Cultural Experiences
